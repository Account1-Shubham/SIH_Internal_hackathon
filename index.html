About SIH & This Internal Hackathon

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide initiative (Ministry of Education / AICTE) that invites student teams to build product-oriented solutions to real-world problems. RJIT is conducting an internal SIH round to shortlist teams for SPOC nomination to the SIH 2025 portal.

Quick links: SIH official site • Idea PPT template • Problem statements

Rules & Regulations

Key points from SIH 2025 guidelines

  • Only students selected in the internal hackathon may be registered on the SIH portal by the college SPOC.
  • Team composition: exactly 6 members; at least one female member is mandatory.
  • SPOC will nominate the top 45 teams (+5 waitlist) from RJIT for SIH.
  • Each team must nominate a team leader (official point of contact) and submit the idea on the SIH portal using the required idea template / PPT.
  • Project submission for the SIH portal requires the Idea PPT and a link to the design/artifact (as per SIH instructions).
  • Any differing/extra rules (stipends, travel, deployment of winner projects) will follow SIH official guidelines and ministry instructions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the internal hackathon?
All RJIT students can form teams and participate. Teams must have exactly 6 members with at least one female member.
What happens after the internal hackathon?
Top teams will be nominated by the SPOC for registration on the official SIH 2025 portal.
When is the deadline for registration?
Register as soon as possible. The internal hackathon is scheduled for 26-27 September 2025.
Do I need to prepare anything before the hackathon?
Review the problem statements, form your team, and prepare using the official SIH idea presentation template.
What support will be provided during the hackathon?
Technical mentors and organizing committee members will be available to guide teams throughout the event.
